Help Hank help...

Your workflow

Sometimes finding the information you need to assign all of the proper codes and modifiers to a case can be time consuming.

Let Hank help! Send Hank the record, and he'll scan it for you and provide a list of the most likely codes and modifiers highlighting the text that contributed to his decisions.

In return, you will help Hank by correcting him when he gets it wrong and explaining with free text why his selection was incorrect. In this way, Hank will learn from his mistakes in near real time.
