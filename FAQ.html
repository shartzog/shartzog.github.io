Who is Hank?

Hank is a cognitive automation solution for medical coders.

What is cognitive automation?

Cognitive automation is a form of artificial intelligence that utilizes feedback from human experts to improve its decision making in near real time.

Why should I work with Hank?

Hank is unique amongst his peers. He will reward you for your efforts at training him. You can choose between receiving cash now or receiving a portion of the profits generated by the model you helped to train.

How will Hank generate a profit?

When Hank has obtained enough feedback, he will be as proficient as his human trainers at selecting the correct medical codes for a given case. At that point, he will be deployed in place of some medical coders.

Why shouldn't I work against solutions like Hank?

Solutions like Hank are, for all intents and purposes, inevitable. AI is progressing quickly, and Hank is the only player in the game that is prioritizing the people whose jobs will be displaced above selfish profits.

How can I get involved?

Hank is still in the development phase, but he'll be available for the masses early this summer. Sign up here to be one of our first users!

What are the 'content contribution requirements' for becoming a contributing user?

Each time you provide quality feedback to Hank, you'll earn credit toward your monthly user costs. Once you've provided enough quality feedback, your monthly costs will be reduced to zero. But that's not the end of the story. You can continue providing feedback to earn cash back or participate in our profit sharing program. In addition, Hank may store a portion of the records you've submitted in a fully deidentified format for future training purposes.

How will I provide feedback to Hank?

Your feedback will be in the form of a free text explanation, but you will be provided with standard phrasing to use when correcting codes to ensure Hank understands what he got wrong. Your adherence to our suggested phrasing will be a part of how your feedback is rated.

Is Hank HIPAA compliant?

Yes. Hank goes to great lengths to ensure that no protected health information is compromised. Records are checked locally and stripped of PHI prior to cloud processing and then checked and stripped again with even more powerful algorithms after they are received by our HIPAA compliant servers.

Can I install Hank even if I'm not an administrator?

Yes. Hank was built to be light weight and does not require administrator access to install as long as you already have the Google Chrome browser.

What operating systems does Hank support?

Hank supports any operating system capable of running the Chrome browser, so basically everything.
