Help Hank help...

Your workflow

Sometimes finding the information you need to assign all of the proper codes and modifiers to a case can be time consuming.

Let Hank help! Send Hank the record, and he'll scan it for you and provide a list of the most likely codes and modifiers highlighting the text that contributed to his decision.

In return, you will help Hank by correcting him when he gets it wrong and explaining with free text why his selection was incorrect. In this way, Hank will quickly learn from his mistakes in near real time.

Your bottom line

Hank's not hiding anything. Eventually, he'll have gathered enough data from his users to get the codes correct as often as his human trainers. Oddly enough, that's what we should all be working toward. Why?

Because when Hank profits, you, his trainers, profit as well. For up to 7 years, you will be paid a portion of the profits generated by any model you helped to train.

Pursuing your dreams, or getting a head start on retirement? That's something we should all be able to get behind.
